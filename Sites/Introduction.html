Introduction and Info to my Site

Every topic will be structured as follows: The very top of the page will be a simple overview of and introduction to the topic, followed by a few examples to deepen your understanding of what you just read.
The second half of the page will be structured the same way as the first half, however presented rigorously according to the formal definition of the topic. It might also include some extra information to broaden the topic to its fullest extent, which normally would go beyond the scope of the subject.
At the very end of a section there will be a list of exercises to deepen understanding, with the answer key right below.
This system works just fine for more advanced topic because it assumes knowledge from the previous tiers, however starting with something basic like numbers or arithmetic operations, it's hard to present it in a simplified manner, but that is luckily negligible considering anybody capable enough to navigate a website and understand the text written will also understand basic arithmetic.

After finishing a Tier you can check out the corresponding Cheat Sheet and Exam to test your knowledge.
